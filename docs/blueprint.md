# **App Name**: CampusConnect

## Core Features:

- Homepage Content Display: Display key information including college introduction, highlights, and quick links to important sections, using content from the user's college content document.
- About Us Section: Showcase the college's mission, vision, and establishment details.
- Courses Listing: Display the list of offered courses with brief descriptions.
- Faculty Showcase: Highlight faculty members and their expertise, attracting both prospective students and teaching staff.
- Admission Details: Provide detailed admission procedures, required documents, and important dates, enabling students to apply directly.
- Facilities Overview: Illustrate available facilities like the library, labs, and sports areas, enticing prospective students.
- Contact Information Display: Clearly display college address, email, and phone number.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#30475E), to convey trust, stability, and knowledge.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E2E8F0), to provide a clean and calm base.
- Accent color: Gold (#D6AD60), used sparingly for highlighting important information and CTAs, symbolizing excellence.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for readability.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, for a modern, computerized look. The Space Grotesk font is used for headings, while the PT Sans font is used for body text.
- Simple, professional icons for different sections (courses, faculty, admissions, etc.)
- Clean and structured layout for easy navigation.
